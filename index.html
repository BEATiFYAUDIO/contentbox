What ContentBox is not

  • Not a forced marketplace
  • Not a custody service

Mission

ContentBox exists to make platforms optional. We build creator-owned infrastructure so publishing, sharing, and getting paid can happen without gatekeepers, opaque rule changes, or lock-in.

Creators should control access, terms, and settlement. The modern internet centralized creation into platforms that can throttle reach, change rules, or remove access without recourse.

  • Own the source — content lives with the creator, not a platform.
  • Share directly — links over feeds, relationships over ranking.
  • Clear terms — permission and entitlements are explicit.
  • Honest settlement — designed for optional sound-money rails.

ContentBox keeps the delivery path simple and predictable. Access rules and settlement hooks attach cleanly, without turning playback into a protocol project.

  • Local-first creation, direct links for access
  • Identity-aware entitlements (wallet optional)
  • Auditability for splits, invites, and approvals
  • Optional payments and receipts that do not block delivery

Why build this way

These are not just technical decisions. They are decisions about who holds power, who bears risk, and who benefits when systems change. ContentBox is built to reduce chokepoints and keep creators closer to their audience on creator-defined terms.

No gatekeepers

  • Publishing and sharing should not require permission from platforms.
  • Distribution should not depend on algorithms or opaque policy changes.
  • Creators can choose discovery later without surrendering control now.

Clear terms, honest settlement

  • Access rules are explicit and creator-defined.
  • Payments are intentional, not manipulative.
  • Settlement can be audited and verified.

Local-first

  • Creators keep custody of their content.
  • No mandatory cloud and no required central host.
  • Works with one node; peers are a bonus.

Direct links

  • Links over feeds, relationships over ranking.
  • Access can be granted without platform accounts.
  • Downloads are intentional and permissioned.

Inspectable infrastructure

  • Integrity and auditability are first-class.
  • Proofs and events can be exported and verified.
  • Source code and docs are public.

How it works

ContentBox replaces platforms with direct links. Creators publish locally, share intentionally, and deliver access on clear terms. Payments and downloads are optional, explicit, and creator-defined.

Publish

  • Creators package content locally.
  • Integrity data enables verification and caching.
  • Pricing and access policies attach to each release.

Resolve

  • Listeners open a direct link.
  • ContentBox locates the creator without a global index.
  • Remote access can be configured when desired.

Deliver

  • Once access is permitted, delivery is immediate and seekable.
  • Previews and entitlements can gate access when configured.
  • Downloads are intentional and permissioned.

FAQ

Do buyers need to install ContentBox?

No. Buyers can access content from a direct link under the creator’s terms. Installing ContentBox is for creators and power users.

Is media stored on-chain?

No. Blockchain (optional) is for payments and receipts only. Content delivery uses standard networking.

Is this DRM?

No heavy DRM. ContentBox focuses on integrity, permission, and auditability.

What about derivatives and clearance?

Derivatives can be previewed privately by authorized stakeholders but require clearance before public release.
