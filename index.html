North Star

I create content → entitlement decides access → it plays.

  • Local-first: content lives with the creator.
  • P2P-ready: resolve creators without a platform intermediary.
  • Verifiable delivery: hash-addressed manifests enforce integrity.
  • No lock-in: no marketplace dependency, no custody capture.

Why ContentBox

Platforms optimize for capture. ContentBox optimizes for exit. It exists for creators who want to distribute work without surrendering control, identity, or economic agency.

Ownership stays local

Your files, your machine, your keys. Distribution does not imply custody.

Links respect rights

Playback follows policy. Access follows entitlement. Nothing is implied as free.

Built for sovereign creators

No algorithmic dependency. No forced marketplace. No extractive middle layer.

How it works

Play

Playback streams efficiently, but access is enforced by entitlement — not assumption.
